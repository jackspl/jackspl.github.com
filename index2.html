About ZhiHong

The company is committed to providing China's manufacturing enterprises with professional services in the field of informatization and digitalization, including big data mining, user behavior analysis system construction, IT monitoring system construction and professional operation and maintenance

Advanced technical framework

Powerful customization technology

Support personalized customized development, customized workflow, project management, coding rules for the enterprise, increase the flexibility of the system.

PLM system implementation capability
